AnesthAssist is an educational reference tool for licensed anesthesiology professionals. It is not intended for patient diagnosis or clinical decision-making.

Overview

AnesthAssist provides anesthesia residents and attending physicians with quick access to reference calculators, emergency protocol checklists, pharmacology references, and ACGME case logging. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AnesthAssist approved for clinical decision-making?

No. AnesthAssist is an educational reference tool for licensed anesthesiology professionals. It is not FDA-cleared or approved for clinical decision support. All calculations and references must be verified against your institution's protocols and current package inserts before any clinical use.

Does the app store patient data?

No patient identifiers or protected health information (PHI) are stored. Case logs are stored locally on your device only and contain only the data you choose to enter. Do not enter identifiable patient information into the app.

I found an error in a drug dose or protocol. How do I report it?

Email us with the specific screen, the value you believe is incorrect, and your source reference. We review all content reports and update the app accordingly. Always verify critical values against authoritative references such as package inserts and ASA/SOAP guidelines regardless of any displayed value.

How do I delete my case log data?

All data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, uninstall the app. Individual cases can be deleted from the Cases tab using the trash icon on each entry.
